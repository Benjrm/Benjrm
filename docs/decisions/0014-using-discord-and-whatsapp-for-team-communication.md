# Using Discord and WhatsApp for Team Communication

## Context and Problem Statement

The team needs a way to communicate throughout the project, covering both structured sprint meetings and quick informal exchanges.

## Decision Outcome

Discord is used as the primary platform for sprint meetings, providing voice channels for video and audio calls as well as persistent text channels for meeting notes and follow-ups.

WhatsApp is used for quick, informal day-to-day communication such as short coordination messages and time-sensitive notifications.

Both tools are already used by all team members in their daily lives, so no onboarding or new accounts are required.
